About Us

Your trusted partner in large format slab installation

Our Story

With over 15 years of experience in the industry, Porcelain has established itself as a leading provider of large format slab installation services in Canada. Our team of certified professionals specializes in handling the most challenging and prestigious projects across the country.

Our Expertise

We specialize in the installation of large format porcelain and ceramic slabs, offering comprehensive solutions for both residential and commercial projects. Our expertise includes:

  • Kitchen countertops and islands
  • Bathroom surfaces and shower installations
  • Commercial spaces and retail environments
  • Outdoor applications and pool surrounds

Why Choose Us

  • Certified installation team
  • State-of-the-art equipment
  • Quality materials and workmanship
  • Comprehensive project management
  • Attention to detail
  • Customer satisfaction guarantee

Our Process

  1. Initial consultation and site assessment
  2. Detailed measurements and templating
  3. Material selection and approval
  4. Professional installation
  5. Quality inspection
  6. Final walkthrough and customer satisfaction
